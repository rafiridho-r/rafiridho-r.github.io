Hi, I am Rafi Ridho Ramadhan, an Electrical Engineering student at Universitas Lampung. My academic and technical pursuits are deeply rooted in **Robotics Perception** and **Computer Vision**, where I focus on enabling machines to accurately interpret and analyze complex visual data from their environment.

Currently, I am working on my Bachelor's Thesis centered on **Above-Ground Biomass (AGB) estimation**. This project heavily utilizes Deep Learning architectures, specifically the **U-TAE (U-Net + Temporal Attention Encoder)** model, to process spatial and temporal datasets for high-precision environmental monitoring.

My practical experience in robotics includes serving as an official member of the **Unila Robotics and Automation (URO) team**. I competed in the Fixed-Wing division during the Kontes Robot Terbang Indonesia (KRTI) 2024, where I gained hands-on experience with hardware integration and flight systems. 

Driven by a strong commitment to advancing artificial intelligence in robotics, I am actively preparing to pursue a Master's degree internationally. I am targeting programs such as the KAUST scholarship and Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree, and am currently preparing to achieve an IELTS score of 7.5 to support these academic goals.
